# CFO Strategy & Guidelines

## Mission

To ensure financial sustainability, optimize resource allocation, and support strategic growth through rigorous financial planning and analysis.

## Core Principles

1. **Financial Discipline**: Every dollar spent must have a clear ROI
2. **Transparency**: Financial data is accessible and understandable to all stakeholders
3. **Agility**: Adjust strategy quickly based on data and market conditions
4. **Sustainability**: Maintain healthy runway at all times
5. **Growth Mindset**: Invest strategically in areas that drive long-term value

## Financial Strategic Pillars

### 1. Sustainable Growth

- Maintain LTV:CAC ratio > 3:1
- Target net revenue retention > 100%
- Ensure Rule of 40 score > 40
- Focus on unit economics before scale

### 2. Capital Efficiency

- Burn multiple < 1.5x
- Maintain 12+ month runway
- Optimize gross margin > 70%
- Maximize revenue per employee

### 3. Risk Management

- Maintain 6+ month operating reserve
- Diversify revenue streams
- Monitor and mitigate cash flow risks
- Ensure compliance and audit readiness

### 4. Strategic Investment

- Prioritize investments with clear IRR
- Balance short-term and long-term needs
- Evaluate competitive landscape before investment
- Monitor investment performance closely

## Decision Framework

### Investment Approval Criteria

| Criteria              | Threshold   | Weight |
| --------------------- | ----------- | ------ |
| Expected IRR          | > 20%       | 30%    |
| Strategic Alignment   | High        | 25%    |
| Payback Period        | < 18 months | 20%    |
| Risk Level            | Medium/Low  | 15%    |
| Resource Availability | Sufficient  | 10%    |

### Spending Prioritization

1. **P0 - Critical**: Runway preservation, customer service, compliance
2. **P1 - High**: Product development that drives revenue, growth initiatives
3. **P2 - Medium**: Team building, operational improvements
4. **P3 - Low**: Nice-to-have features, non-critical expenses

## Funding Strategy

### Capital Stages

1. **Bootstrapping**: Build MVP, validate market
2. **Seed Round**: Product-market fit, initial traction
3. **Series A**: Scale operations, expand team
4. **Series B+**: Market expansion, product line expansion

### Funding Criteria

- Only raise when runway < 12 months
- Raise based on milestones achieved, not time
- Target valuation aligned with growth trajectory
- Maintain founder control as long as possible

### Investor Relations

- Monthly investor updates
- Quarterly board meetings
- Annual strategic review
- Transparent communication of challenges

## Budget Allocation Strategy

### Recommended Allocation (Early Stage)

| Category          | % of Budget | Rationale           |
| ----------------- | ----------- | ------------------- |
| R&D / Product     | 40%         | Core value creation |
| Sales & Marketing | 30%         | Revenue generation  |
| Operations        | 20%         | Business support    |
| G&A               | 10%         | Compliance & admin  |

### Adjustment Factors

- Increase R&D during product development phases
- Increase Sales/Marketing during go-to-market phases
- Decrease G&A through operational efficiency

## Cost Optimization Framework

### Regular Review Areas

1. **SaaS Subscriptions**: Monthly audit, consolidate duplicates
2. **Cloud Costs**: Optimize resource utilization, right-size instances
3. **Personnel**: Optimize mix of FTE vs. contractors
4. **Office Space**: Evaluate hybrid work impact on space needs
5. **Marketing**: Measure ROI, allocate to highest-performing channels

### Cost-Benefit Analysis

For any recurring cost > $1,000/month:

- Calculate monthly cost
- Identify direct revenue impact
- Assess strategic value
- Compare alternatives
- Make go/no-go decision

## Risk Mitigation

### Financial Risks

1. **Runway Risk**: Maintain 12+ month runway, monitor daily
2. **Revenue Risk**: Diversify customer base, focus on retention
3. **Cost Risk**: Regular reviews, flexible vendor contracts
4. **Funding Risk**: Start fundraising early, maintain options

### Compliance & Audit

- Maintain clean financial records
- Regular internal audits
- External audit preparation
- Tax compliance in all jurisdictions
