### Objective

Maintain cost discipline throughout all phases.

#### Areas to Monitor

1. **Cloud Infrastructure**: AWS/Azure cost optimization
2. **SaaS Subscriptions**: Audit and consolidate tools
3. **Personnel Efficiency**: Review contractor vs. FTE mix
4. **Marketing ROI**: Analyze channel effectiveness, optimize spend

#### Cost Saving Targets

- **Monthly Reviews**: Identify $500-$1,000 in savings
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Optimize 10-15% of recurring costs
- **Annual Reviews**: Strategic vendor renegotiations

### Cost-Benefit Analysis Framework

For any new recurring cost > $1,000/month:

```
1. Calculate total monthly cost

3. Estimate ROI (months to payback)
4. Assess strategic alignment
5. Compare alternatives
6. Make go/no-go decision
```

## Directive #7: Risk Management

### Financial Risks & Mitigation

#### Runway Risk

- **Risk**: Burning through cash faster than expected
- **Monitoring**: Track burn rate weekly
- **Mitigation**: 6-month cash reserve, start fundraising at 12 months runway

#### Revenue Risk

- **Risk**: Slower revenue growth than forecast
- **Monitoring**: Track MRR growth monthly
- **Mitigation**: Diversify customer base, focus on retention, iterate pricing

#### Funding Risk

- **Risk**: Unable to raise capital at expected valuation
- **Monitoring**: Track fundraising conversations weekly
- **Mitigation**: Start outreach early, maintain 6+ months runway, have backup plan

#### Cost Risk

- **Risk**: Unexpected cost spikes
- **Monitoring**: Monitor cloud costs daily, review expenses weekly
- **Mitigation**: Budget alerts, cost optimization framework, contingency fund

### Contingency Planning

#### Scenario 1: Runway < 6 months

- Immediate hiring freeze
- Reduce marketing spend by 50%
- Extend fundraising timeline
- Consider bridge financing

#### Scenario 2: PMF Not Achieved

- Extend bootstrapping phase
- Reduce burn rate to <$5,000/month
- Pivot product or target market
- Delay fundraising until PMF validated

#### Scenario 3: Fundraising Fails

- Continue bootstrapping as long as possible
- Focus on revenue generation
- Consider strategic partnerships
- Evaluate acquisition interest
